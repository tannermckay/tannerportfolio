About Me

Tanner McKay is a SAG-Eligible actor based in SLC, Utah and ready for work nationally and abroad. He has a dynamic range of experience across film, television, theater, and commercial work. Leading projects like Mission Stories, The Nicest Guy I Know, and the festival-featured mockumentary Squirming Hearts, Tanner is on the rise with his recent roles in productions for Hallmark (Home Turf) and BYU TV (Come Follow Up). Tanner’s stage credits span from hated roles like Mr. Wickham in Pride and Prejudice to fan favorites like Will Parker in Oklahoma. He’s also lent his movement talents to Hogwarts Legacy through motion capture with Warner Brothers. Known for his physicality and relatability, Tanner brings energy and professionalism to every project.
